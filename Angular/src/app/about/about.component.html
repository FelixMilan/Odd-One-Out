ABOUT US!

Retail parody was dreamt of and created by Felix, Jason, Jordan and Tudor for our 2022 Personal Development module. Our project title was "Odd-One-Out Game", so we strove to create a game built off the success of many other games in the genre: fun interactivity, party environment specialist, easy to pick up and most importantly: funny!
